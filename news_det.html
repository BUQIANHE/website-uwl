How To Get The Most Out Of 3 Days In Iceland

It is deeply rooted from its founder, River Lin’s childhood dream of establishing a company with a sole goal of delivering happiness to people around the world.

What triggered him carrying out his dream was due to his unforgettable experience in Germany. During his first trip there in April 2003, he was attracted by the beauty of household gardens and was impressed by the harmony between people and the nature. As he walked by a pathway, he was greatly touched by the warm smile of an old lady watering her plants in her garden and the joyful laughter from children playing around trees and flowers in the backyard. He suddenly realized that this was exactly the true happiness he ever wanted to deliver to people, and it would be fabulous if he could help more people live happily with the nature beauty in the gardens.
